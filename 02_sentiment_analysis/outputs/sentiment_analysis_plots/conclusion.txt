SUMMARY AND CONCLUSION

Sentiment as a predictor of S&P 500 returns:
- Sentiment index was NOT significant in the simple linear model (p = 0.9159 )
- Sentiment index was NOT significant in the multiple linear model (p = 0.9535 )
- Sentiment index ranked 4 out of 6 in Random Forest importance
- Sentiment index ranked 2 out of 6 in XGBoost importance

Best performing model: Linear (Sentiment Only) with test RMSE of 0.009208 

OVERALL CONCLUSION:
Sentiment appears to have predictive value for S&P 500 returns,
though its importance varies across different modeling approaches.
